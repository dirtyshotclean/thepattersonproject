The Patterson Project Massacre

"On June 24th, 2022, a group of young filmmakers vanished while shooting a documentary deep within Camp Nelson, California. What happened remains a mystery."

Background

The crew, working on a project known only as "The Patterson Project," entered the remote woods to capture local legends and ghost stories on film. Three days later, they were reported missing.

Evidence Found

  • Abandoned tents
  • Half-recorded tapes
  • Disturbing hand-drawn symbols on trees
  • A single blood-stained journal

Official Statement

"After an exhaustive search, no bodies have been recovered. Foul play is suspected, but no suspects have been identified. We urge the public to avoid the Camp Nelson woods until further notice."
- Tulare County Sheriff's Department, July 5, 2022

Unanswered Questions

Was it a staged hoax? A cult? Something far more terrifying? The truth has never been revealed. Only fragments of their final hours remain...
